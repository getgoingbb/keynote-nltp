Bob Goff Speaking Fee: What to Expect

Researching the Bob Goff speaking fee for your conference? Here's what published listings suggest, why his message about trust resonates, and how to decide whether a celebrity-tier fee fits your event's goals.

Typical Fee Range

Published speaker-bureau listings generally place Bob Goff's keynote fee at $50,000 and up, with the final number shaped by date, location, format, and exclusivity. As always, bureau ranges are directional — confirm with his team.

Why Event Planners Book Bob Goff

Goff is the New York Times bestselling author of Love Does, Everybody Always, and Dream Big. His storytelling builds immediate trust with an audience — whimsical, generous, and disarmingly personal. For faith-adjacent gatherings, leadership retreats, and culture-focused events, he's a reliable standing ovation.

Building Trust From the Stage

The through-line in Goff's work is that trust is built through presence and small acts done consistently. If that's the outcome you want — an audience that leaves more connected to each other and your organization — prioritize speakers who model the message rather than just describing it.

When a Different "Wow" Wins

For wellness, healthcare, senior-living, and active-aging audiences, the most trust-building thing a speaker can do is show what's possible. That's a different budget tier — and often a deeper impact.

An Unforgettable Alternative for Wellness & Aging Events

At 85, Stephen Jepson doesn't just talk about vitality — he juggles, balances, and knife-throws his way through the most talked-about keynote your attendees will ever see. Ideal for healthcare, senior living, corporate wellness, and active-aging conferences.
